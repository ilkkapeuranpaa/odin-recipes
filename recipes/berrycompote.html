Warm Berry Compote

Description

Cook frozen berries with orange juice in your slow cooker for a yummy dessert by itself or over ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups frozen mixed berries
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons finely grated orange zest
  • ¼ cup orange juice
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
